fuguefm
—

A live-coded language for DSP and patterns — sound and score in one file, right in your browser. Write a patch, hit play, and shape it while it sounds — the engine hot-swaps your edits without dropping a sample.

fugue grew from one idea — combining Faust's DSP algebra with Tidal's patterns — and it stands on the shoulders of giants: SuperCollider, TidalCycles, Max/MSP & Pure Data, and many more before them. Thank you.

Made by Nikhil Bhanu, aided & abetted by Claude.
